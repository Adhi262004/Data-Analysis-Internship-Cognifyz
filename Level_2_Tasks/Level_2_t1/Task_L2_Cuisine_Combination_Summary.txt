### Level 2 – Task 1: Cuisine Combination Analysis

**Objective**:
- Identify the most common cuisine combinations.
- Analyze which combinations receive higher average ratings.

**Approach**:
- Grouped full `Cuisines` combinations as they appear.
- Counted how often each combination appears.
- Calculated the average rating for each combination.
- Visualized the Top 10 combinations using a horizontal bar chart.

**Top 3 Cuisine Combinations**:
1. North Indian, Chinese — 902 restaurants, Avg Rating: 3.9
2. North Indian — 777 restaurants, Avg Rating: 3.95
3. Chinese — 612 restaurants, Avg Rating: 3.86
